Discover the Basil Pesto

Vegan and Gluten Free

Pesto is an Italian paste traditionally made with leaves of genovese basil, extra virgin olive oil, Parmesan, pecorino sardo, pine nuts, and garlic

Martha Stewart is getting out of prison, so today the terror alert was raised from orange to pesto

- David Letterman

Ingredients:

  • Toasted pine nuts: 1/2 cup
  • Fresh lemon juice: 2 tablespoons
  • Garlic Clove: 1
  • Sea Salt: 1/4 teaspoon
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Basil leaves: 2 Cups
  • Extra-virgin olive oil: 1/4 Cup
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese,: 1/4 Cup

Preparation:

  1. In a food processor, combine the pine nuts, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper and pulse until well chopped.
  2. Add the basil and pulse until combined.
  3. With the food processor running, drizzle in the olive oil and pulse until combined. Add the Parmesan cheese, if using, and pulse to briefly combine. For a smoother pesto, add more olive oil.
